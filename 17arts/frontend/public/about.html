Our Story
About 17ARTS
We believe every wall deserves a story. 17ARTS was born from a passion to connect India's extraordinary artists with collectors who appreciate the rare and the beautiful.
Our Mission

To democratise access to authentic Indian art — making it easy for collectors everywhere to discover, buy and cherish original artworks directly from the artists who create them.

100% Authentic
Every artwork comes with a signed certificate of authenticity from the artist.
Safe Delivery
Professional art packaging ensures your artwork arrives in perfect condition.
Artist First
Artists receive the majority of every sale. We exist to support their livelihood.
Who we are
Based in Hyderabad, India

Founded in 2020, 17ARTS started as a small curated gallery in Hyderabad. Today we work with over 340 artists across India, spanning traditional, contemporary, folk and heritage styles.

Our team of art experts personally reviews every artwork before it's listed, ensuring quality and authenticity for every collector.
